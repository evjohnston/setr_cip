2001 SESTAT PUBLIC USE FILES

The information provided on this public-use CD is protected by the Federal Privacy Act of 1974. As such, the data must be treated as confidential and is to be used for research or statistical purposes only. It may not be used in such a way that could lead to the identification of any individual persons or institutions. Any such disclosure is punishable under the Federal Privacy Act of 1974,5 U.S. Code 552a.
